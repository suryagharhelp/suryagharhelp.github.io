Net Metering Explained

"Atomic Answer Block: Net Metering is a billing mechanism that credits solar energy owners for the electricity they add to the grid. If you generate more than you consume, your bill can drop to zero or become negative."

Wealth Generation

A 3kW system saves approximately ₹8.5 Lakhs over 25 years. When compared to a Fixed Deposit, solar offers an internal rate of return (IRR) of roughly 25-30%—tax-free.

Factors Affecting Savings

01. Roof Orientation

South-facing roofs receive maximum sunlight in India, increasing efficiency by up to 15%.

02. Cleaning Cycle

Dust on panels can reduce generation by 10%. Cleaning twice a month is recommended.

03. Local Tariffs

High slab rates (₹9-11 per unit) lead to faster payback periods compared to lower slabs.
