2026 Policy Insights

What is the subsidy for a 3kW solar system?

As per the **Surya Ghar Solar Subsidy 2026** updates, a 3kW system receives a total of ₹78,000. This is broken down as ₹30,000 per kW for the first 2kW (₹60,000) and ₹18,000 for the 3rd kW.

Why is 3kW called the "ROI King"?

The subsidy structure caps at 3kW. While you can install 5kW or 10kW, the government subsidy does not increase beyond ₹78,000, making 3kW the most financially optimized installation for residential users.
