PM Surya Ghar Subsidy Calculator India 2026

PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana is one of India's biggest rooftop solar initiatives designed to help households lower electricity bills and generate clean power from their own rooftops. Through this scheme, eligible homes can receive financial support for installing solar systems.

Our calculator helps estimate how much subsidy you may receive based on usage patterns and likely solar capacity. It also estimates annual savings and payback period.

How Subsidy Works

Generally, smaller households may need 1kW to 2kW systems, while larger households with higher monthly electricity usage may require 3kW or more systems. Central subsidy can go up to ₹78,000 depending on latest policy slabs.

Who Should Apply?

  • Homeowners with rooftop space
  • Families paying regular electricity bills
  • Users planning long-term savings
  • People wanting clean renewable energy

Main Benefits

Rooftop solar can reduce dependence on rising electricity tariffs, improve long-term savings, increase property value and provide environmental benefits.

Required Documents

Usually Aadhaar card, electricity bill, bank account details, rooftop ownership proof and address proof may be required. Visit our documents page for full checklist.
