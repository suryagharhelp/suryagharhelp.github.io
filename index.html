What is PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana?

PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana is a Government initiative launched to help Indian households install rooftop solar systems and reduce monthly electricity bills. Under this scheme, eligible residential users can receive subsidy support for installing solar panels on their rooftops. This helps families save money, generate clean energy, and become less dependent on rising electricity costs.

The main goal of this scheme is to encourage solar energy adoption across India. With increasing electricity demand and higher power bills, rooftop solar is becoming one of the smartest long-term investments for homeowners. Through PM Surya Ghar Yojana, families can lower their expenses and also support green energy development.

How Does PM Surya Ghar Subsidy Work?

Under the scheme, subsidy is generally provided based on the capacity of the rooftop solar system installed. Many users search for 1KW, 2KW, and 3KW solar subsidy amounts because these are common household system sizes.

  • 1KW Solar System – Suitable for low monthly electricity use
  • 2KW Solar System – Good for small families
  • 3KW Solar System – Best for medium households
  • Higher KW Systems – Useful for large homes with more usage

The exact subsidy amount depends on current government rules, approved vendor installation, and eligibility conditions. Our calculator above helps estimate benefits quickly.

Who Can Apply for PM Surya Ghar Scheme?

This scheme is mainly for residential households in India. If you own a home with usable rooftop space and have an active electricity connection, you may qualify based on current rules.

  • Indian residential households
  • Valid electricity meter connection
  • Suitable rooftop area
  • Required documents available
  • Approved vendor installation

Documents Required

During online application, users commonly need the following documents:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Latest Electricity Bill
  • Bank Account Details
  • Address Proof
  • Passport Photo
  • Property Ownership Proof (if required)

How to Apply Online for PM Surya Ghar Subsidy

  1. Visit official PM Surya Ghar portal
  2. Select your state and electricity board
  3. Register with mobile number
  4. Enter consumer number details
  5. Upload required documents
  6. Choose approved vendor
  7. Install rooftop solar system
  8. Inspection and subsidy processing
