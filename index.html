PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana 2026

PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana is a rooftop solar support scheme focused on helping Indian households reduce electricity expenses through solar energy adoption. Eligible residential users may receive subsidy support for installing rooftop systems.

This calculator gives a practical estimate based on common household consumption. Final subsidy may vary depending on latest government rules, approved vendor process, system size and state-level implementation.

Who Can Benefit?

  • Families with rising monthly electricity bills
  • Homes with usable rooftop area
  • Long-term savers wanting lower utility cost
  • Users interested in renewable energy

Common System Sizes

1kW suits low users, 2kW suits small families, 3kW suits medium homes and higher systems suit heavy users.

Required Documents

Commonly needed items include Aadhaar card, electricity bill, bank details and ownership proof. Visit the Documents page for full checklist.
