PM Surya Ghar Yojana 2026 Guide

What is PM Surya Ghar Scheme?

PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana is an Indian rooftop solar scheme that helps residential households install solar systems with government subsidy support.

3kW Subsidy Amount

A 3kW rooftop solar system can receive subsidy up to ₹78,000 based on current benchmark structure.
