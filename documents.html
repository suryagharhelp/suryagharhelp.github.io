Documents Required for PM Surya Ghar Subsidy

Prepare these documents before applying for rooftop solar subsidy under PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana. Correct documents help faster approval, fewer rejections and smooth subsidy transfer.

Aadhaar Card

Primary identity proof of applicant. Name should match electricity bill and bank account where possible.

Latest Electricity Bill

Required to verify consumer number, DISCOM details, sanctioned load and active domestic connection.

Bank Passbook

Needed for subsidy transfer through Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT). Keep account number and IFSC clear.

Roof Ownership Proof

Sale deed, tax receipt, mutation copy or other ownership proof may be needed in some cases.

Passport Photo

Recent clear passport size photo may be required during profile or vendor process.

PAN Card (Optional)

Sometimes useful for financing, loan processing or vendor KYC depending on provider.

Full Checklist Before Applying

Mandatory in Most Cases

  • Aadhaar card of applicant.
  • Latest electricity bill.
  • Mobile number linked for OTP verification.
  • Bank passbook / cancelled cheque.
  • Residential address details.

May Be Asked Depending on State / DISCOM

  • Property ownership proof.
  • NOC from joint owner.
  • PAN card.
  • Rooftop photo.
  • Load enhancement request if required.
  • Old meter replacement documents.

Important Name Matching Rule

Applicant name on Aadhaar, electricity connection and bank account should preferably match. Mismatch can delay verification or subsidy payment.

File Upload Tips

  • Use clear PDF or JPG scans.
  • Avoid blurry photos.
  • Keep file names simple.
  • Upload front page of passbook with IFSC.
  • Bill should be recent and readable.

How These Documents Are Used

  • Electricity bill verifies connection.
  • Aadhaar confirms identity.
  • Bank proof enables DBT subsidy transfer.
  • Ownership proof confirms rooftop rights.
  • Photo supports profile verification.

Pro Tip for Faster Approval

Before submitting application, keep all files ready in one folder. Double-check consumer number, IFSC code and spelling of your name. Many applications get delayed because of small mistakes.

FAQ

Is Aadhaar compulsory?

Usually identity verification is required, and Aadhaar is commonly used.

Can I use someone else's bank account?

Best practice is applicant's own bank account to avoid payment issues.

What if electricity bill name is old owner?

You may need ownership proof or name correction with DISCOM first.

Can tenants apply?

Usually ownership / rooftop rights matter. Check local rules and approvals.
