Quick Fact: PM Surya Ghar Subsidy for 3kW System

As of April 2026, the PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana subsidy for a 3kW solar system is capped at ₹78,000. This is calculated as ₹30,000 per kW for the first 2kW, plus ₹18,000 for the third kW. For systems above 3kW, the subsidy remains fixed at the ₹78,000 maximum cap.

Why 3kW is the ROI King in 2026

In the current Indian residential solar market, the 3kW rooftop solar system has emerged as the most financially viable option. With panel efficiencies of N-Type TOPCon technology reaching 25%, a 3kW setup generates approximately 360-450 units monthly, effectively eliminating electricity bills for the average 2BHK or 3BHK household.

The Payback Logic

With the central subsidy covering roughly 40% of the initial ₹1.8 lakh investment, most homeowners in high-sunlight regions like Delhi, Rajasthan, and Gujarat see a complete return on investment within 4 to 5 years. Considering these systems are warranted for 25 years, you enjoy over two decades of truly "Muft Bijli."

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the subsidy disbursement take?

Under the 2026 digital workflow, the subsidy is credited via Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) within 30 days of the commissioning certificate being issued by your DISCOM.

Can I combine state subsidies with PM Surya Ghar?

Yes! States like Rajasthan offer an additional ₹8,000 per kW, and Uttar Pradesh provides ₹15,000 for specific categories. Use our calculator's state dropdown to see stacked benefits.
