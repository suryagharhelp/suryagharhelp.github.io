If you are planning to apply under the PM Surya Ghar Muft Yojana, keeping all required documents ready can save time and avoid rejection. Many users face delays because uploaded files are unclear, names do not match, or bank details are wrong.

This page explains all commonly required documents for PM Surya Ghar subsidy application in India. Requirements may vary slightly depending on state DISCOM and latest portal updates.

Important: Always use the latest official portal and check your local electricity provider rules before final submission.

1. Aadhaar Card

Aadhaar is usually the primary identity document. The name on Aadhaar should ideally match the electricity bill and bank account name.

Tips:

  • Use clear front-side scan/photo
  • Check spelling of name
  • Use updated mobile linked Aadhaar if OTP required

2. Latest Electricity Bill

The electricity bill helps verify your active domestic connection. It also shows your monthly usage, which helps estimate suitable solar system size.

Make Sure Bill Shows:

  • Consumer Number
  • Meter Number
  • Applicant Name
  • Address
  • Recent billing month

3. Bank Account Details

Approved subsidy amount is generally transferred directly to the beneficiary bank account. Incorrect account number or IFSC may delay payment.

Recommended Upload:

  • First page of passbook
  • Cancelled cheque
  • Account holder name visible

4. Rooftop Ownership / House Proof

Since subsidy is for rooftop solar installation, the applicant should usually have ownership or legal permission for the rooftop area.

Possible proofs may include:

  • Property tax receipt
  • House deed copy
  • Municipal document
  • NOC if shared property

5. Passport Size Photograph

Some users may need to upload a recent passport size photograph depending on portal workflow or installer verification.

Common Reasons for Rejection

  • Blurred uploads
  • Name mismatch in Aadhaar and electricity bill
  • Wrong consumer number
  • Inactive bank account
  • Unreadable passbook image
  • Old electricity bill
  • Wrong state DISCOM selected

Best Practice Before Uploading

  1. Keep all files in one folder
  2. Rename files clearly (aadhaar.pdf, bill.pdf)
  3. Use good lighting for photos
  4. Crop unnecessary background
  5. Double check spellings
  6. Confirm account details carefully

For Assam Users

If you are applying from Assam, keep your APDCL / local DISCOM electricity bill ready. Ensure consumer number is entered exactly as shown on bill.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I apply without Aadhaar?

Usually Aadhaar is required for identity verification. Check latest portal rules.

Can bank account be in another person's name?

Preferably applicant's own account. Mismatch may cause delay.

Can tenant apply?

Depends on rooftop ownership permission and DISCOM rules.

Is PAN Card compulsory?

Not always, but requirements may vary.

Final Advice

The fastest applications are submitted by users who prepare all documents beforehand. Before starting PM Surya Ghar subsidy registration, scan your Aadhaar, latest electricity bill, and bank passbook clearly.

Correct documents = faster approval + smoother subsidy release.
