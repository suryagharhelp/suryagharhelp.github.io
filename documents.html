Documents Checklist 2026

1 Identity & Address

  • ✅ Aadhaar Card (Must be linked to mobile)
  • ✅ Latest Electricity Bill (Not older than 3 months)

2 Financial Data

  • ✅ Cancelled Cheque or Passbook (For DBT Subsidy)
  • ✅ PAN Card (Optional but recommended)

Pro-Tip for 2026:

Ensure the name on your Electricity Bill matches the Bank Account name exactly. Mismatches are the #1 cause of subsidy rejection.
