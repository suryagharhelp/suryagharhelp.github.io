Documents Checklist 2024

1 Identity & Address Proof

  • ✅ Aadhaar Card: Must be linked to an active mobile number for OTP verification.
  • ✅ Latest Electricity Bill: Must be recent (not older than 6 months) and in the applicant's name.

2 Bank & Financial Details

  • ✅ Cancelled Cheque or Passbook: To verify bank details for Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) of the subsidy.
  • ✅ PAN Card: While optional for some cases, it is highly recommended to have it ready.

Pro-Tip for 2024:

Ensure the name on your Electricity Bill, Aadhaar Card, and Bank Account match exactly. Even a small spelling mismatch is the #1 reason for subsidy application rejections.
