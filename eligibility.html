Eligibility Criteria 2026

Ensure you meet these requirements before applying for the subsidy.

Core Requirements

  • ✅ Indian Citizenship: The applicant must be a citizen of India.
  • ✅ Domestic Connection: You must have a residential electricity connection. Commercial users are not eligible.
  • ✅ Suitable Roof: You must own the house or have a No-Objection Certificate (NOC) from the landlord. The roof should be suitable for solar panel installation.
  • ✅ No Prior Subsidy: The household should not have availed any other solar subsidy from MNRE previously.

Common Disqualifiers

  • ❌ Rented Property: Applying without a formal NOC from the property owner.
  • ❌ Commercial Use: Your electricity connection is registered for commercial or industrial purposes.
  • ❌ Incorrect DISCOM: Selecting the wrong electricity distribution company during registration.
