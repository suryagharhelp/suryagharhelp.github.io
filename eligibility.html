PM Surya Ghar Eligibility 2026

PM Surya Ghar scheme supports Indian households installing rooftop solar systems.

Who Can Apply?

  • Indian residential households
  • Valid electricity connection
  • Suitable rooftop space
  • Bank account for subsidy transfer
  • Required ID documents

Important Notes

Eligibility may vary based on DISCOM rules and latest government updates.
