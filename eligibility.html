PM Surya Ghar Eligibility 2026

Find out who can apply for rooftop solar subsidy, what conditions matter, and how to improve your chances of approval.

Apply Online Guide

Main Eligibility Conditions

Many Indian households may qualify if they meet residential, rooftop and electricity connection requirements.

Residential Household

The scheme is mainly intended for domestic / residential electricity users.

Active Electricity Connection

You should have a valid consumer number and active billing account.

Suitable Rooftop

Your roof should have usable space with sunlight for panel installation.

Correct Documents

Aadhaar, bill and bank details should be accurate and readable.

Ownership / Permission

Homeowners usually have smoother approval. Shared roofs may need consent.

Approved Process

Installation and subsidy flow may require approved vendors or technical checks.

Detailed Eligibility Guide

PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana aims to support rooftop solar adoption across India. While many homes may qualify, final approval usually depends on technical feasibility, documentation and local DISCOM process.

1. Domestic Electricity Users

The program generally focuses on residential consumers. Commercial shops, factories or industrial users may fall under different rules.

2. Rooftop Space & Sunlight

Solar systems need enough roof area and proper sunlight exposure. Even smaller roofs may support 1kW or 2kW systems.

3. Meter & Bill Status

Keep electricity bills updated. Incorrect consumer details, inactive accounts or pending issues may slow the process.

4. Owner Occupied Houses

Owner occupied houses generally have easier documentation because rooftop rights are clear.

5. Tenants / Rented Homes

Tenants may need owner approval since rooftop installation affects property structure and ownership rights.

6. Joint Family / Shared Property Cases

If property ownership is shared, additional NOC or consent may be required depending on the case.

Common Reasons for Delay or Rejection

  • Name mismatch in Aadhaar and bill
  • Wrong consumer number
  • Roof not suitable
  • Unreadable uploaded files
  • Ownership dispute
  • Incorrect bank details

Best Way to Improve Approval Chances

Use correct documents, updated electricity bill, accurate bank details and choose a practical system size based on monthly usage.

For Assam Applicants

Applicants from Assam should keep local DISCOM details ready and verify consumer number exactly as printed on the bill.
