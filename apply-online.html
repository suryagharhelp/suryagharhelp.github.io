Apply Online: 5-Step Process

Follow this sequence to ensure your ₹78,000 subsidy is approved without delays.

1

Portal Registration

Visit pmsuryaghar.gov.in and select your State and Electricity Distribution Company (DISCOM). Use your consumer number as shown on your bill.

2

Feasibility Approval

Wait for DISCOM approval. This step confirms your local transformer can handle the extra solar load. This usually takes 3-7 business days.

3

Vendor Installation

Once approved, select a Registered Vendor. Ensure they use ALMM-approved panels and provide a 5-year system warranty.

4

Net-Metering & Inspection

The vendor will install the bi-directional meter. A final inspection is conducted by DISCOM to generate the commissioning certificate.

5

Subsidy Claim

Submit your bank details and a cancelled cheque on the portal. The subsidy is typically released to your account within 30 days of commissioning.

Why Applications Get Rejected

  • ❌ Name mismatch between Electricity Bill and Aadhaar.
  • ❌ Uploading blurred photos of the installation site.
  • ❌ Choosing a non-registered vendor for installation.
  • ❌ Incorrect Bank Account (IFSC) details for DBT transfer.
