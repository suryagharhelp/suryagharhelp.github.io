Apply Online: 5-Step Process

Follow this sequence to ensure your ₹78,000 subsidy is approved without delays.

1

Portal Registration

Visit pmsuryaghar.gov.in and select your State and Electricity Distribution Company (DISCOM). Register using your mobile number and electricity consumer ID.

2

Feasibility Approval

Wait for DISCOM approval. This step confirms your local transformer can handle the extra solar load. This usually takes 3-7 business days. You'll receive approval via SMS.

3

Vendor Installation

Once approved, select a Registered Vendor. Ensure they use ALMM-approved panels and provide a 5-year system warranty. The vendor will handle all installation logistics.

4

Net-Metering & Inspection

The vendor will install the bi-directional meter. A final inspection is conducted by DISCOM to generate the commissioning certificate. This typically takes 1-2 weeks.

5

Subsidy Claim

Submit your bank details and a cancelled cheque on the portal. The subsidy is typically released to your account within 30 days of commissioning. Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) ensures direct payment to your account.

Why Applications Get Rejected

  • ❌ Name mismatch between Electricity Bill and Aadhaar.
  • ❌ Uploading blurred photos of the installation site.
  • ❌ Choosing a non-registered vendor for installation.
  • ❌ Incorrect Bank Account (IFSC) details for DBT transfer.
