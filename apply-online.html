Detailed Apply Online Guide 2026

PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana helps households install rooftop solar systems with subsidy support. The application process is generally digital, allowing users to register, upload documents and track progress.

Step 1: Prepare Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Latest Electricity Bill
  • Bank Passbook / Cancelled Cheque
  • Mobile Number
  • Address / Ownership proof if needed

Step 2: Use Correct Personal Details

Your name, mobile number and consumer number should be entered accurately. Small mistakes often cause delays.

Step 3: Select Correct DISCOM

Each applicant must choose the correct state electricity provider. Wrong selection may stop processing.

Step 4: Upload Clear Files

Use readable PDF or JPG files. Avoid dark, blurry or cropped images.

Step 5: Choose Proper System Size

1kW, 2kW or 3kW depends on your monthly usage and roof size. Use our homepage calculator for a quick estimate.

Step 6: Wait for Technical Review

Authorities or vendors may verify connection details, rooftop feasibility and documents before installation.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Wrong consumer number
  • Name mismatch in documents
  • Unreadable uploads
  • Incorrect bank details
  • Old electricity bill
  • Wrong state selected

How Long Does Approval Take?

Approval time varies by state, demand volume, documentation quality and inspection schedule.

For Assam Applicants

Keep local DISCOM details ready and verify consumer number exactly from your electricity bill.

Best Tip for Faster Approval

Upload clean documents, double-check all entries and use active bank details linked to your records.
