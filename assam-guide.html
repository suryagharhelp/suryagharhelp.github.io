Assam Solar Guide 2026

APDCL Application Process

For residents of Assam, the designated utility for processing PM Surya Ghar applications is the Assam Power Distribution Company Limited (APDCL). The process is the same as other states, but ensure you select APDCL as your electricity distribution company.

The APDCL portal integrates with the national PM Surya Ghar system, ensuring seamless verification and subsidy disbursement.

Apply via APDCL Portal

Key Points for Assam Applicants

  • ✅ Verify APDCL Consumer Number: Verify your APDCL consumer number from a recent electricity bill. This is crucial for portal registration.
  • ✅ Name Matching: Ensure the name on the bill matches your Aadhaar card exactly.
  • ✅ No State Additional Subsidy: Assam currently offers no additional state-level subsidy, but you get the full central assistance of up to ₹78,000.
  • ✅ Fastest Approvals: The fastest approvals are for standard 3kW rooftop solar installations.
