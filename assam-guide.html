Assam Solar Guide 2026

APDCL Application Process

For residents of Assam, the designated utility for processing PM Surya Ghar applications is the Assam Power Distribution Company Limited (APDCL). It's crucial to ensure your electricity connection is registered under the "Domestic" category to be eligible. Our 2026 data indicates that Assam has one of the fastest approval rates nationwide, especially for 3kW systems, making it an ideal time to apply.

Apply via APDCL Portal

Key Points for Assam Applicants

  • ✅ Verify your APDCL consumer number from a recent electricity bill.
  • ✅ Ensure the name on the bill matches your Aadhaar card.
  • ✅ Assam currently offers no additional state-level subsidy, but you get the full central assistance.
  • ✅ The fastest approvals are for standard 3kW rooftop solar installations.
