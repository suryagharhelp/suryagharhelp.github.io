PM Surya Ghar Assam Guide

Residents of Assam can benefit from rooftop solar subsidy under PM Surya Ghar scheme subject to electricity board and portal process.

Useful for Assam Users

  • Reduce electricity bill
  • Use rooftop solar system
  • Receive subsidy benefit
  • Long term savings

How to Apply

Use national portal and select Assam electricity provider.
